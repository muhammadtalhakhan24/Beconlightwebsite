HISTORY OF BEACON LIGHT ACADEMY

Beacon Light Acedemy were established towards the end of the twentieth century (1986). With a rich history of thirty years (30 years), the School leads as a symbol of educational aspiration for students with a special purpose of providing quality education at an affordable price. At a time, when government allocation and spending was minimal on the education sector, BLA established itself as one of the leading education providers in the city.

The first branch of BLA opened in Gulshan-e-Maymar, with a vision of providing quality education. Since then, the School system has established firmly in this region, and has further diversified in areas such as Gulshan-e-Iqbal and Gulzar-e-Hijri.

BLA is indebted to its founding members, who are still committed to play a role in the ongoing process of social enlightenment of this nation’s youth. The founding members are committed to produce socially responsible gradates who would prove to be open minded, religiously enlightened and successful citizens of Pakistan.

The BLA is build on an everlasting notion, “Enter to learn, go forth to serve the Nation”. Today, when the nation requires bright and talented individuals to carry its torch of success, Beacon Light Academy is providing just the raw material required for the development.
