SPORTS IN BLA

The importance of sports and games in school encompasses more than just the benefit of physical activity. It increases self-esteem and mental alertness making school sports and games necessary for every school age child. Sports teaches students the value of humility. Competitions teach them how to accept win or defeat and how to start new.We have our own football team which play with many teams and win many matches.

We at BLA provide both indoor and outdoor sporting activities for our children of every age. Sports at BLA include:

  • Cricket
  • Hockey
  • Basket ball
  • Foot ball
  • Thrown ball
  • Badminton
  • Table tennis
  • Volley ball
