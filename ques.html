FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q1. How many subjects do you enroll for O-Levels?

Ans. At least 9 subjects. 5 compulsory and four optional.

Q2. Can any of your students appear for Matriculation exams from your school?

Ans. No! The School offers only IGCSE Exams (EDEXCEL U.K Qualification).

Q3. What is the difference between the EDEXCEL Board and the Cambridge Board?

Ans. The EDEXCEL Board is an International Board which is conducting exams in its home, i.e. U.K as well as all over the world while the Cambridge Board is conducting its exams only in South Asia and does not exist even in its own Home Land.

Q4. When do the admissions start? What is the procedure for getting admissions?

Ans. Admissions start from the first working day of March. The procedure involves two ways, i.e. Online application or coming in person to the office and getting your child registered. A photograph of the applicant is compulsory. The admission test date and timing would be announced. Admission would be given purely on merit basis.

Q5. What is the age for admission?

Ans. For nursery the child must be between 3 1/2 to 4 1/2 years on the 1st of August of the session. In the same way, for admission to class one, the child should be between 5 1/2 to 6 1/2 years on the 1st of August and so on.
